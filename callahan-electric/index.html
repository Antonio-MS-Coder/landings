What we do

01 The specialty

Generator installs

Central Texas weather doesn't ask permission. A standby generator, installed right — transfer switch, dedicated circuits, a clean handoff when the grid drops — means the AC hums and the fridge stays cold while the neighbors dig out the candles.

02

Panel upgrades

Crowded panels, tired breakers, no room for what your house runs now. Upgraded capacity, labeled clearly, with space to grow.

03

Troubleshooting

Dead outlets, flickering lights, a breaker that won't hold. Traced with a meter and a method — not guesswork and parts-swapping.

04

Lighting

Recessed, exterior, task, fixture swaps — layouts planned on purpose and switched where your hand expects them to be.

05

New construction & remodels

Rough-in through trim-out, wired to code and coordinated with the other trades — for new builds and for walls coming down.
